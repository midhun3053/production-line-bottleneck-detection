## KEY FORMULAS TO REMEMBER

```
Bottleneck Score = 
  (Cycle_Time × 0.4) + 
  (Waiting_Time × 0.3) + 
  (Downtime × 0.2) + 
  (Defect_Rate × 0.1)

High Score = Bad (Bottleneck)
Low Score = Good (Efficient)
```
